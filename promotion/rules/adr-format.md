# ADR Format Rules

## ADR location
- Store ADRs in `docs/adr/`.
- Use filenames like `0001-short-title.md`, `0002-short-title.md`.

## ADR template
Use this structure:

# ADR-XXXX: Title

## Status
Proposed | Accepted | Superseded

## Context
What problem or constraint exists?

## Options considered
- Option A
- Option B
- Option C

## Decision
What was chosen?

## Consequences
What becomes easier, harder, or riskier?

## Notes
Links to related docs, tickets, or diagrams if useful.

## Guidance
- Keep ADRs short.
- Prefer one decision per ADR.
- Record why the decision was made, not just what changed.
